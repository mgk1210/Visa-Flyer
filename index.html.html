Why this matters
Visa's total compensation package is exceptionally rich — but its complexity creates real tax traps. RSUs vesting in November hit your W-2 at under-withheld rates. Deferred comp distributions can collide with other income. ESPP shares trigger different tax treatment depending on hold period. Without a coordinated plan, you may be paying tens of thousands more in taxes than necessary each year.
